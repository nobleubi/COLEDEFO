Transforming Lives Worldwide

COLEDEFO is dedicated to supporting less privileged women and girls through education, and healthcare access. By providing essential skills and resources, the program helps them break the cycle of poverty, build confidence, and achieve independence. Coledefo creates safe spaces, fosters leadership, and ensures every woman and girl has the opportunity to thrive and succeed.

OUR TRACK RECORD

COLEDEFO are passionately committed to uplifting women and the girl child through our dedicated charity initiative...

We believe in creating an environment where women can thrive, access opportunities, and achieve their fullest potential. Our programs focus on education, skill development, and health, ensuring that every woman and girl has the tools and support needed to succeed.

In addition to our core initiatives, we have actively worked on various impactful projects....

We've provided free medical care to those in need, ensuring access to essential health services. We have also supported children and women with disabilities by providing crutches, wheelchair and mobility aids, ensuring they can move with greater ease and independence. Our organization has also donated food items to children, and those in need ensuring they have access to nutritious meals... etc.

Through collaboration and community engagement...

we strive to break barriers and build a future where gender equality is the norm. We aim to foster a world of opportunity and empowerment for all.
