We have been supporting children for over 100 years and we won't stop now.

    Save the Children saves lives and is cost effective. 85% of every dollar goes straight to our mission.
  • Around the world, we provide newborn health services, malnutrition screening and treatment, and help prevent disease.
  • When disasters strike, Save the Children responds to the unique needs of children to ensure their survival.
  • We have a long-standing partnership with the U.S. Government, which includes actively working with several Administrations to ensure that children are given the best start in life.
    • With your support, we can continue our work to keep children in the U.S. and around the world healthy, educated and safe.
