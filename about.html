ABOUT COLEDEFO

Women empowerment has been a long-standing issue, with which many women around the world are facing challenges. Despite significant progress in recent decades, disparities still exist in areas such as education, healthcare, economic participation, and political representation.

We at COLEDEFO have bridged this barrier through empowerment with skill acquisition and trainings. Our concerns for the development of women and the girl child are based on long-term benefits and results that are proving to be beneficial globally.

Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission

We are a charity organisation whose sole purpose is to liberate women and the girl child from poverty in our communities and beyond.

Our Vision

To be at the forefront of selfless services, innovative and sustainable programs with global best practices.

Our Goals

Goal #1 – Education

Ensuring equal access to quality education for girls.

Goal #2 – Healthcare

Access to healthcare services including maternal health.

Goal #3 – Economic Opportunities

Promoting entrepreneurship and financial independence.

Goal #4 – Political Representation

Encouraging women’s leadership and participation.
