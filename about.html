ABOUT COLEDEFO

Coledefo is a dynamic organization dedicated to addressing the unique needs of women and the girl child. Our mission is to create a safe, inclusive space where women can access essential resources, education, and opportunities for personal and economic growth. At LACDEF, we believe that when women and girls are empowered, entire communities thrive. We focus on promoting gender equality, providing skill acquisition programs, supporting education for the girl child, and advocating for women’s rights. Through mentorship, leadership training, and community outreach, we equip women with the tools to break barriers and achieve their full potential. LACDEF remains committed to uplifting women and girls, ensuring they become confident, independent, and impactful members of society.
