Fulfilling Our Mission

COLEDEFO is committed to fulfilling its mission of supporting less privileged women and the girl child through impactful programs. By providing access to education, vocational training, mentorship, and healthcare, EWI helps women overcome social and economic barriers.

The organization fosters self-reliance, leadership, and confidence, enabling women to build sustainable futures. Through community outreach and strategic partnerships, EWI ensures lasting change, transforming lives and creating opportunities for generations of women and girls to thrive and lead empowered lives.

OUR TRACK RECORD

COLEDEFO are passionately committed to uplifting women and the girl child through our dedicated charity initiative. We believe in creating an environment where women can thrive, access opportunities, and achieve their fullest potential. Our programs focus on education, skill development, and health, ensuring that every woman and girl has the tools and support needed to succeed.

In addition to our core initiatives, we have actively worked on various impactful projects. We've provided free medical care to those in need, ensuring access to essential health services. We have also supported children and women with disabilities by providing crutches, wheelchairs, and mobility aids, ensuring they can move with greater ease and independence. Our organization has also donated food items to children and those in need, ensuring they have access to nutritious meals.

Through collaboration and community engagement, we strive to break barriers and build a future where gender equality is the norm. We aim to foster a world of opportunity and empowerment for all.

PARTNERSHIP INVOLVEMENT

COLEDEFO is committed to championing the rights and opportunities of women and the girl child. Our mission is to break down barriers and create a world where every woman can thrive with equal rights and access to opportunities.

Our goal is to empower women in rural communities by providing them with the financial means and educational resources they need. We aim to equip them with skills and opportunities that foster independence and self-sufficiency, ensuring they can pursue their dreams without limitations.

We invite you to become a part of our mission. Your financial support can help us expand our programs and reach more women and girls. Additionally, your moral support and advocacy can raise awareness and inspire others to join our cause.

Together, we can build a future where every woman and girl has the chance to shine, achieve her dreams, and contribute to society. Your involvement can help turn this vision into reality.

Our Milestones

Concerned Ladies Empowerment and Development Forum has made a strong impact, especially in Nigeria—covering Abuja, Delta, Lagos, and various rural areas. We've conducted free medical tests for elderly women, provided food supplies to hundreds of widows and young girls, and carried out outreach programs that support women’s basic health and welfare.

In addition, the forum has organized hands-on empowerment programs that have trained and equipped hundreds of women and girls with practical skills. These include tailoring, soap-making, small-scale business training, and basic digital literacy. Through generous donations from individuals and partners, they’ve also distributed startup materials and small grants to help women kickstart their businesses and become financially independent.

Looking ahead, the forum plans to grow its network of donors and sponsors, so they can reach even more underserved communities. Their goal is to empower thousands more through advanced vocational training, leadership development, and access to healthcare and education. They’re also working toward establishing permanent training centers where women can continuously learn and grow.
